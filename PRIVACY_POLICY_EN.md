# Peace - Book of Answers Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to "Peace - Book of Answers" (the "App"). We understand the importance of privacy to you, and therefore have created this Privacy Policy to explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

## 2. Information Collection

### 2.1 Types of Information We Collect

**Local Storage Information:**
- Questions you input
- Answers you receive
- Question and answer history (maximum 1000 records)
- App settings preferences (font selection, answer library selection, etc.)

### 2.2 Information We Do Not Collect

- Personal identification information (name, phone, email, etc.)
- Location information
- Contact information
- Photos and media files
- Privacy data from other applications

## 3. Information Usage

### 3.1 Purpose of Use

We collect and use your information solely for the following purposes:

- **Core Functionality**: To provide you with Q&A services and answer history
- **User Experience Improvement**: To provide personalized settings based on your preferences
- **App Optimization**: To analyze app usage for product improvement
- **Technical Support**: To help resolve technical issues and provide customer support

## 4. Information Storage

### 4.1 Storage Location

- All user data is stored locally on your device
- We do not upload your data to any servers

### 4.2 Storage Duration

- Q&A history: Maximum 1000 records, automatically deletes oldest records when exceeded
- App settings: Permanently stored until you actively delete the app
- Temporary files: Deleted immediately after use

## 5. Information Sharing

### 5.1 We Do Not Share Your Information

We commit to not sharing, selling, or disclosing your personal information with any third parties, including:

- Advertisers
- Data analytics companies
- Other app developers
- Any business partners

## 6. System Services

### 6.1 System Services

This app may use the following system services:

- **Photo Library Access**: For saving answer wallpapers (only when you actively use this feature)
- **Share Function**: For sharing answer content (only when you actively use this feature)

### 6.2 Permission Management

- All permission requests will clearly explain the purpose of use
- You can revoke permissions at any time in system settings
- Denied permissions will not affect the app's core functionality

## 7. Privacy Policy Updates

### 7.1 Update Notifications

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be notified through the app or other means.

### 7.2 Effective Time

Updated privacy policies will take effect immediately upon publication. Continued use of this app indicates your agreement to the updated policy.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the following methods:

- **In-App Feedback**: Through the "About Us" page in the app
- **Email**: leilei0091@icloud.com

We will respond to your inquiries as soon as possible after receiving them.
